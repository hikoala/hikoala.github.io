Bundles

At Koala, we chose to gather our products and services in bundles. A bundle is a package gathering one or several products at once and dealing with possible product incompatibilities.

Example: You can have a bundle called Silver containing a Flight Disruption Compensation product and a bundle called Gold containing both Flight Disruption Compensation and Flex products.

Each time you ask Koala for a quote, you will receive one quote per bundle from us.

Bundles are generated by Koala and are the result of business talks between your company and Koala.

Bundle identifier‚Äã

Each bundle has a unique technical name that will NEVER change. Every quote will be identified by its related bundle name.

You can therefore rely on the bundle name to simply identify the products behind the bundle.

For instance, the bundle name will be essential to retrieve our product T&C.

Our bundles will deal with any possible product incompatibilities. Let's take a closer look at what this means üîé

Imagine you want to offer 3 products to your customers, 1 included by default in all bookings (called Koala Flex) and 2 optional which are exclusive products (called Flight Disruption Compensation and Missed Connection Compensation).

In such a case, you will have 3 bundles:

  • one bundle Silver containing Koala Flex only
  • another bundle Gold containing both Koala Flex and Flight Disruption Compensation
  • and a last bundle Platinum containing both Koala Flex and Missed Connection Compensation
