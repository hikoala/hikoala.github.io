Bundles

At Koala, we chose to gather our products and services in bundles. A bundle is a package gathering one or several products at once and dealing with possible product incompatibilities.

Example: You can have a bundle called Silver containing a Flight Disruption Compensation product and a bundle called Gold containing both Flight Disruption Compensation and Withdrawal products.

Each time you ask Koala for a quote, you will receive one quote per bundle.

Bundle definition#

Bundles are managed by Koala and result from business talks.

tip

In the staging environment, we will set arbitrary pricing for a bundle when the actual pricing has not yet been contractually defined. We will update the bundles pricing as soon as the prices are final between Koala and you.


Bundle identifier#

Each bundle has a unique technical name that will NEVER change. Every quote will be identified by its related bundle name.

Bundle example#

As mentioned above, our bundles will deal with any possible product incompatibilities. Let's take a closer look at what this means ðŸ”Ž

Imagine you have 3 products, 1 included by default in all bookings (called Koala Flex), and 2 optional which are exclusive products (called Flight Disruption Compensation and Missed Connection Compensation). In such a case, you will have 3 bundles:

  • one bundle Silver containing Koala Flex only
  • another bundle Gold containing both Koala Flex and Flight Disruption Compensation
  • and a last bundle Platinum containing both Koala Flex and Missed Connection Compensation
