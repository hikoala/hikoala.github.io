Bundles

Koala chose to represent their products and services as bundles. A bundle if a way of bundling one or many products at the same time and deal with products exclusivity.

For example, you could have two bundles, one bundle called "Silver" containing a Flight Disruption Compensation product and a bundle "Gold" containing both a Flight Disruption Compensation product and a Withdrawal product.

Each time you ask Koala for a quote (see next section), you will receive one quote per passenger per bundle.

Bundle definition#

For now, bundles are managed by Koala and result from commercial discussions.

tip

In the 'development' environment, it is possible that you'll receive an arbitrary pricing for a bundle because the actual pricing was not contractually defined when your environment was created. We try to update bundles as soon as the contracting and the prices are final between Koala and you.

Bundle Identifier#

Bundles have a technical unique name you can rely on which will NEVER change. Every quote will be identified by it's related bundle id. You can find the exhaustive list of your bundles by calling

curl <>/parner/bundles
> [
{
"id": "bozk"
},
{
"id": "flux"
}
]

Bundle#

If order to deal with product exclusivity, we chose create a bundles for all combination of products. Imagine you have 3 products, one you always want included (A), and two optional but exclusive products (B1 and B2). In this case, we will have created 3 bundles:

  • a bundle bozk with A only
  • a bundle flux with A and B1
  • a bundle zrof with A and B2

This will make more sense after reading the next section dedicated to quotes.
